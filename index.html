Welcome to Baby's First CTF!

A Beginner's Guide to Simple Capture-The-Flag Challenges!

Never completed a CTF before? No worries, Baby's First CTF is meant to walk you through some of the basic CTF challenges that commonly come up in competitions. Each level consists of a walkthrough and a "try it yourself" question. No need to registration, no stress, just enjoy learning!

What even is a CTF?

A "CTF" stands for "Capture the Flag", referring to cybersecurity exercises where participants attempt to find text strings called "flags", purposefully hidden. This allows individuals to learn and practice cybersecurity skills, improve problem-solving abilities, and receive more hands-on training.

How does it work?

  • Learn key concepts that are common in CTF challenges.
  • Read the walkthrough problems for further understanding.
  • Try solving the sample problem in the level.
  • Expand your Cybersecurity knowledge!
